Fruit Salad Recipe

Ingredients

Apple
Banana
Mango
Grapes
Orange
Pomegranate
Papaya
Watermelon
Lemon Juice

Preparation Steps

  1. Wash all the fruits properly.
  2. Peel fruits such as Banana, Mango and Orange.
  3. Cut all the fruits in small pieces.
  4. Add them into a large bowl.
  5. Mix gently without crushing the fruits.
  6. Add Lemon Juice if required.
  7. Chill for a few minutes.
  8. Serve fresh.
